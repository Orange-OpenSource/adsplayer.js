### Release Notes v1.0.0 (2016/05/11) 
* Based on Mast specification 0.9 and Vast specification 2.0
* Implements preroll digital video advertising. Can play several successive ads supplied by one or several Vast.
* Supports ads video format which can be handled by the browser like mp4
* Supports also ads image formats jpeg, gif and png
* Can consider the clickthrough link allowing user click on ad (video or image)
